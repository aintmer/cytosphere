Cytosphere

Twelve generative wallpaper patterns drawn from real biology, chemistry, and physics. Rendered natively at up to 16K. No AI. No tracking.

For iPhone, iPad and Mac · Requires iOS 17 / macOS 14

Features

Wallpapers that mean something.

Every pattern is a procedural composition. Same look, same seed → same wallpaper. Change the seed, get an entirely new arrangement. Twelve patterns × infinite variation = a wallpaper you've actually never seen before.

🧬

Twelve generative patterns

Mitosis, hematopoiesis, atomic orbitals, viral capsids, electric fields, molecular structures, and more. Everything procedural — no stock images.

🎨

Fine-grained controls

Hue, saturation, density, depth-of-field, element scale, opacity. Live preview re-renders instantly as you tweak. Save your favorites as presets.

📐

Up to 16K export

Pick a square ratio for desktop or portrait for iPhone/iPad lock screens. Standard 6K, High 10K, or Ultra 16K — even on a Pro Display XDR you'll have pixels to spare.

🔒

Privacy-first

No accounts. No ads. No analytics. The app makes no network connections of its own — every wallpaper is rendered entirely on your device.

⚡️

Native Swift renderer

Pure CoreGraphics. No Electron, no web view, no Metal compute kernels. Tiny app, fast launch, low battery cost.

♾️

One-time unlock

Two patterns free forever — Mitosis and Sketch Organelles. Unlock the remaining ten with a one-time purchase. No subscription, ever.

Cytosphere runs entirely on your device. No backend, no analytics, no telemetry. The app makes no outbound network connections of its own — the only thing that talks to Apple's servers is the standard in-app-purchase verification, handled by iOS / macOS itself.
